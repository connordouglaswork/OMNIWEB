About

OMNI Athletics is a web based platfrom that provides human performance science training tools, information and applications. OMNI Athletics is designed to be used by athletes, individuals, coaches, nutiritionist, scientists and more. The platform was created by 2 brothers who share a love for health, sports, technology, science and community.

Connor Douglas



Connor is a double major scientist specializing in both Human Performance Science and Computer science. He holds both a Bachalors degree for both these fields and a diploma in web development. Connor has over 10 years experience working with athletes and trains at a high level himself. Growing up he has competed in basketball, tennis, crossfit, weightlifting and more. Currently he is training to beat to Guiness World Record for the worlds heaviest pull up and will be attmepting a 110kg pull up in 2024. Connor loves seeing people reach their goals and with the help of his brother has launched this platform to provde ease of access to high level training tools and knowledge.
