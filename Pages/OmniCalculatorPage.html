OMNI Athletics Calculator

About:

The OMNI Calculator is designed for sport scientists, strength and conditioning coaches, nutritionists and athletes to quantify, record and optimize their performance. The calculator offers six calculation modes strength, power, anaerobic, aerobic, nutrition and biomechanics. Each mode has a set of different calculations that are relevent to their designated field. The calculations are stored in the calculators history where they can be reviewed, analysed and managed.
