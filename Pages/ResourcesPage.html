Athlete Resources

Resources approved by OMNI Athletics.

Nutrition Resource

MyFitness Pal

My fitnesspal is a nutririon resource that allows for easy daily tracking of calories, macro and micro nutrients. This allows athletes to see if they met nutrient specific goals such as daily caloric intake, protein requirement, vitamin sufficiency. My fitnesspal had both a padi and free version, witht he free version offering many great features that make it easily on of the best fitness applications on the market.

Supplement Resource

NZ Muscle

NZ Muscle is a New Zealand fitness brand that sells supplements, clothing and gym gear. They are the largest supplier of sporting supplements in New Zealand and the range, price and quality of their product beats their competitors. OMNI Athletics highly recommends athletes in New Zealand use NZ Muscle when shopping for supplements to boost their performance.

Training Resource

BodyBuilding.com

BodyBuilding.com is a historical body building site filled with years of information on training plans, movement tips, nutrition advice and so much more. Have any fitness questions? then check out BodyBuilding.com.

Equipment Resource

Torpedo 7

Maybe you don't ahve a gym member ship, thats fine. You can train at home and build your own home gym with the help of Torpedo 7. Torpedo 7 offers high quality training gear for great prices. They sell power racks, barbells, ergometers and more.

HIT Resource

CrossFit

CrossFit has alot of fans and haters. Regardless CrossFit offers high intensity workouts that are going to challange anyones anaerobic and aerobic systems. If you are searching for a high intensity workout check out what CrossFit has to offer.

Pharmacology Resource

T Nation

OMNI Athletics recommends T-Nations forum when searching for anecdotal information surrounding pharmacology. OMNI Athletics does not promote the use of performance enhancing drugs due to the health impacts, we do however recognise that some athletes require medication for medical conditions. Managing niche health issues is challanging as information is limited. T-Nations forums will provide a great data bank for these athletes to help manage their health.
